Who We Are

Equis is Ghana's premier car rental and automotive service provider, established in 2009 with a vision to revolutionize the transportation and vehicle maintenance industry. What started as a small fleet of 20 vehicles in Accra has grown into a comprehensive automotive service network spanning four major cities.

We pride ourselves on offering not just car rentals, but a complete automotive solution. Our state-of-the-art repair and maintenance facilities are staffed by certified mechanics who ensure every vehicle in our fleet and every customer's car receives the highest standard of care.

Today, we serve over 10,000 satisfied customers annually, maintaining a fleet of 500+ premium vehicles and operating modern service centers equipped with the latest diagnostic and repair technology.
